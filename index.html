About this curriculum
Life Skills Framework
5 interconnected life skills that build future-ready students
Why this curriculum

GrowthMinded is built on the premise that academic success alone isn't enough. Students need the emotional, social, and cognitive skills to navigate real life — and those skills can be taught.

The 5 units are sequenced to build on each other, starting with emotional intelligence as the foundation. Each skill is broken into classroom-ready lessons that take 15 minutes and require no prep.
